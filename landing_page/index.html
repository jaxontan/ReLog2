FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do shared invite codes work?

Simply generate a 6-digit invite code inside your ReLog² album. Share it with your travel partners, and they can instantly join the shared map and drop their live photos and voice logs.

Can I use ReLog² offline while trekking?

Yes. ReLog² cache-buffers your location, photos, and voice notes locally when offline. As soon as your phone connects to Wi-Fi or cellular networks, they sync onto the shared map.

Are confession logs completely private?

Yes. Confessions are encrypted and fully hidden from other group members. They can only be unlocked for group viewing once the album creator ends the trip.
