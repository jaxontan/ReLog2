Pricing Help

Pricing Frequently Asked Questions

Is the $5 Pay As You Go fee a subscription?

No. The Pay As You Go upgrade is a one-time purchase per album. Once upgraded, your entire group joins, maps, and captures memories completely free.

Do my friends need to pay to join my upgraded album?

No. Only the album creator upgrades the album. All other group members can download the app and collaborate on the map completely free.

Can I delete or revoke an album once created?

Yes. You can revoke and delete a newly created album 1 time if no other participants have joined yet. However, once group members have joined and shared memories, deleting the album requires unanimous consent (all group members must vote to agree) to prevent accidental loss of shared travel memories.
