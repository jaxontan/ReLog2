Core Features Explained

1. Collaborative Route Mapping

As your group travels, ReLog² coordinates GPS location pins on a shared, dynamic timeline. Instead of static disconnected snapshots, your journey compiles into an interactive trail mapped directly onto geographical paths.

  • Real-time coordinates sharing with group encryption keys
  • Automatic bezier curving algorithm mapping roads

2. Polaroid Cascade Rain

Watch group uploads settle directly onto their coordinates in real time. The Polaroid Cascade stacks captures sequentially, giving an organic collage representation of active memories that lets group partners immediately see what others uploaded.

  • Staggered physics-based entry animations
  • Dynamic stacking logic avoiding overlapping pin occlusion

3. The Echo Trail Soundscapes

Rather than relying solely on visual maps, the Echo Trail converts nodes into audio hotspots. Group partners drop raw ambient voice recordings, emitting pulsing soundwaves that travel across coordinates. When waves intersect, they flag shared moments.

  • Audio amplitude mapped to canvas ripple radii
  • Multi-node chime generation based on coordinate closeness

4. Time-Locked Confession Vaults

Add private thoughts or anonymous trip feedback in encrypted time vaults during the trip. Secrets remain locked from other group members and are only revealed automatically once the album creator ends the trip.

  • Cryptographic locks tied to target time and status variables
  • End-of-trip decryption triggers

5. HTML5 Scratch-to-Reveal Canvas

The interactive forgotten book sits covered in a simulated layer of dust. By dragging cursors or fingers, users scrape away dust particles, raising the underlying gold title text opacity dynamically from 20% to 100% on threshold clearance.

  • Canvas pixel density scanning for transparency coverage
  • Responsive layout redrawing keeping dust states stable

6. 3D Double-Sided Page Flipping Engine

Pages inside the travelogue operate as double-sided 3D sheets. Clicking pages flips them around a left-anchored spine pivot using CSS 3D transforms (`preserve-3d`), automatically swapping visible content sides based on rotation angles.

  • Backface-visibility hiding for seamless text swapping
  • Anchored left spine pivot (`transform-origin: left`)

7. Web Audio API Synthesizer

Rather than loading massive audio assets, the app features a synthesized Web Audio chime engine. Interactive nodes generate sine-wave audio signals with custom envelope parameters (attack, decay, release) to play musical frequencies.

  • AudioContext oscillators running natively in browsers
  • Dynamic gain-node envelopes preventing audio popping

8. Expanding Shutter Preloader

Site entry is locked behind a dark camera-record overlay. Clicking the red dot triggers a native GSAP animation that scales up the preloader shutter 60x, revealing the landing page beneath in a stylized expanding camera lens transition.

  • Synchronized body scroll unlock on preloader fade
  • Hardware-accelerated GSAP scale and visibility timelines

User & Setup Guides

1. Joining via 6-Digit Room Codes

ReLog² makes setting up collaborative albums instant. The trip coordinator generates a unique 6-digit invite code. Share this token with travel partners to connect their apps immediately without complex login routines.

REL4OG

2. Off-The-Grid Offline Buffering

When hiking or traveling through regions with weak cellular connectivity, the app caches GPS data, photos, and voice notes locally in the sandbox storage. Once connection is re-established, the cache automatically uploads in the background.

3. Printable Layout Book Export

At the end of your trip, click "Export Layout Book" in the album dashboard. ReLog² compiles the route trace, coordinates, photos, and decrypted group confessions into a beautifully formatted PDF book ready to be printed or archived.
